// OUR STORY

From a garage to a movement

NexaBot Labs was founded in 2021 by a group of engineers and educators frustrated by one simple fact: STEM education in Kenya talked about technology but rarely let students touch it.

We set up our first class in Kamakis with 12 students, two Arduino kits, and a lot of optimism. Those 12 students built their first line-following robots in three weeks. Two of them now mentor younger students at the lab.

Today, we run term-based programs, school partnerships, and holiday bootcamps. We have trained over 500 young people and we are just getting started.

// WHAT WE STAND FOR

Our Values

01

Access for All

Robotics should not be a privilege. We keep programs affordable and partner with schools to reach students who wouldn't otherwise have access.

02

Learn by Building

Every concept is taught through a physical project. If a student can't hold it, solder it, or code it — we haven't taught it properly.

03

Fail Forward

When a robot doesn't work, that's where the real learning begins. We celebrate iteration, debugging, and honest effort over perfect results.

04

Local Context

Our projects solve real Kenyan problems — from sensor-based irrigation to smart waste systems. Engineering with purpose and local relevance.
